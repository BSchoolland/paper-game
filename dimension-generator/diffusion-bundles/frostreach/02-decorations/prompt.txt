Dimension: Frostreach
Biome: A whole frozen planet: wind-scoured snow plains, frozen pine forests, splintered glacier peaks, blue ice caves and crevasses, frozen sea-ice, and rare steaming geothermal vents.
Mood: Cold, exposed, and indifferent — beautiful pale light over a world that will bury you without noticing.
Palette: primary #dce8f0, secondary #3a5a78, accent #8fd3df

Description: A frozen planet wrapped pole to pole in snow and old glacier ice, where pale bands of cold light hang over the drifts like halos and nothing in the white offers shelter. Its biomes range from wind-scoured snow plains and frozen pine forests to splintered glacier peaks, blue crevasse caves, and steaming geothermal pockets that never quite thaw. Travelers dig in or die; the world helps no one.

Art style: simple, not too detailed, pencil and crayon drawing, but more pencil than crayon.
